--- title: "Prerequisites" ---
The following list is a non-exhaustive list of the prerequisites for admission to the Msc in Data Science:
  • An Lower Seconds Class degree or better (or equivalent) in Computer Science, Statistics, or Mathematics

    OR

  • An Lower Seconds Class degree in another suitably numerate discipline subject to taking qualifying courses
  • Previous experience in Data Science and/or Statistical Analysis would be an asset

Since we consider candidates with various backgrounds, there will be cases where a candidate may be lacking in one or more skills. In such cases we suggest that the candidate contact us before submitting an application so we can advise them on how they can acquire the missing skills.
